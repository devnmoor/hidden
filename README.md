### Overview
Hidden is designed as a digital escape room experience where observation, accuracy, and fine motor control are essential. The game challenges players to carefully interact with their environment, locate subtle clues, and complete precision-based tasks to unlock puzzle pieces and advance through the rooms.

The experience is designed to be intuitive for first-time players while still offering increasing depth and challenge as the game progresses.

### Gameplay
1. Enter a room and explore the environment
2. Interact with objects to uncover hidden hints
3. Complete mini-games to earn puzzle pieces
4. Collect all puzzle pieces to unlock the next room
   
Each room introduces new mechanics or tighter accuracy requirements, ensuring the gameplay remains fresh and challenging.

### Difficulty Progression
Difficulty increases with each room by adjusting interaction sensitivity and accuracy margins. Early rooms are more forgiving, while later rooms require precise touch control and careful movements, making progression feel rewarding and skill-driven.
